COMPANY

Eurohealth is a service company located in Beijing and works for European medical suppliers in China. We support the launch of medical products in China and concentrate on the following product groups only:

  • pharmaceuticals
  • pharmaceutical ingredients
  • medical instruments
  • medical disposables
  • health food
  • food
  • cosmetics
  • diagnostics

Our business activities serve product launches in China and include:

  • product registration(registration of medical products)
  • partner management(selection and handling of sales partners)
  • sales(sales of medical products)
  • projects(special tasks)

Eurohealth was founded in 1997 and runs a functional office in the German Centre in Beijing. The company is managed by Jens Dreier. Eurohealth employs a qualified team of Chinese with medical and technical know how.
